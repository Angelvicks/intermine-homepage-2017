Disparate data in, unified data out

InterMine integrates disparate biological data sources, making it easy to query and analyse data.

InterMine is open source (LGPL 2.1) and free to use.

There are around 30 InterMines out there. Common genomic and proteomic data sources include X, Y, and Z.
